OPR Terrain Patterns — Property-Based System

9 distinct patterns based on terrain property combinations. Visual language: Dots = Cover, Lines = Difficult, X = Dangerous

1. Open

No properties
Roads, clearings, open ground

2. Impassable + Blocking

Cannot enter, blocks LOS
Buildings, mountains, deep water

3. Cover

+1 Defense when in/behind
Barricades, craters, low walls

4. Difficult

Max 6" movement
Rubble, swamps, shallow water

5. Dangerous

Roll Tough, 1 = wound
Lava, minefields, razor wire

6. Cover + Difficult

+1 Defense, max 6" move
Forests, fields, hills

7. Cover + Dangerous

+1 Defense, wound on 1
Toxic smog, unstable ruins

8. Difficult + Dangerous

Max 6" move, wound on 1
Death marsh, toxic rivers

9. Cover + Difficult + Dangerous

All three properties
Deadly vegetation, carnivorous jungle

Visual Language Summary

Dots / Stipple = Cover (+1 Defense)
Diagonal Lines = Difficult (max 6" move)
X Marks = Dangerous (wound on 1)
Solid Dark = Impassable + Blocking (can't enter, blocks LOS)
Empty White = Open (no special rules)

Elevation is shown separately via contour lines and numeric labels (already implemented).
